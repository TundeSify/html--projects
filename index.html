Chapter 1126: "Taking Responsibility"

In One Piece Chapter 1126, titled "Taking Responsibility," The chapter starts off with the Giants and Straw Hats reminiscing the past Bartolomeo proves his unwavering loyalty to Luffy by drinking poison meant for Luffy after losing to Shanks' crew. However, the poison turns out to be a test, and Bartolomeo's ship is destroyed by Yasopp, leaving his fate uncertain. This chapter also features Blackbeard's crew planning to infiltrate Mariejois, adding tension to the ongoing storyline, and lastly some of the Straw Hat members has disappeared from the Giants Ship, and Nami wakes up in a mysterious Lego like room.
