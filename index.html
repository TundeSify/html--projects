Current Events


Manga

Chapter 1128: "RPG"

In One Piece Chapter 1128, titled "RPG," the Straw Hats continue their adventure in a mysterious land that was initially mistaken for Elbaf. They discover that they are actually in the "Land of Gods," a place ruled by mythological deities, such as the Spike God and the Sun God. The chapter reveals that the island the crew is on is made of LEGO-like blocks. As Luffy and the others explore, they encounter two beasts inspired by Norse mythology: Hilmungandr, a snake-like creature, and Muginn, a raven. Luffy, in Gear 5 mode, defeats Hilmungandr, while Zoro takes down Muginn. The crew then flees as the mysterious Sun God—depicted wearing a deer skull—chases them. The chapter ends with the Straw Hats navigating their way through a diorama-like world as they try to escape, with the Sun God in pursuit.


Anime

Episode 1121: "Garp and Kuzan" - A Master and a Pupil's Beliefs Clash

At the Pirate Island of Beehive, Vice Admiral Garp, who came to rescue the kidnapped Koby, deals with an overwhelming number of pirates alone. As he and his fellow Navy soldiers try to escape the island, Kuzan, the former Navy admiral, stands in their way.
